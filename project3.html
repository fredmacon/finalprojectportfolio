When deciding on a non-profit organization that was local, we chose the HCPLC, or Hillsborough County Public Library Cooperative, as a site that needed help with its overall design. It’s current design was slightly outdated and needed a new way to organize all the information and services offered on it.

Projects Overview

100% online

Teams

HCPLC

Chris B. and I teamed up to redesign the Books and Media page. The colors scheme used was white, light and bright orange, a light and darker blue; agreed upon by the whole team using a mood board. Pictures in each section added to help user find what they were looking for faster while sticking with the scheme for the overall project. We made a desktop and mobile app design.
